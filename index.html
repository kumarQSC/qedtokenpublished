The Process

Step 1

Physical asset created.

Step 2

Asset tokenized on a
Blockchain network.

By transitioning to a digital token system, the asset retains its essential qualities while the asset owner achieves new efficiencies such as increased asset liquidity thanks to the automation of a previously laborious and manual operation.

Step 3

Rules of ownership and other
business logic incorporated.

Apply rules and set limits to control how, and when a token and asset are transferred. Set expirations for items. Gain control and visibility of your product as it traverses the real world.

Step 4

Asset and token transferred.

Step 5

Asset and token acquired.

All tokenized asset transactions are instantly and automatically verified, timestamped, cryptographically encrypted, and added to an immutable distributed ledger that is accessible to investors and asset owners.

Step 6

Asset authenticated through
provenance of token.

The whole history of transactions made involving tokenized assets can be tracked by asset owners and investors on the distributed ledger. This proves the provenance and origin of assets and deters fraud and counterfeiting.

Step 7

Track asset-related notifications
from manufacturer.

The whole history of transactions made involving tokenized assets can be tracked by asset owners and investors on the distributed ledger. Manufactures can know and contact the current owner of an asset that has been transferred from the original buyer.

Step 8

Trade asset in the secondary market.

Sell and buy assets in the secondary market with the authenticity assured.

 

What’s in it for me?

Value to Manufacturer

  • Reinforces the image that a product is highly desirable and likely to be copied by serving as a certificate of authenticity which cannot be counterfeited.

  • Adds to a product's high-tech appeal as a marketing tool linking the product to cutting edge blockchain technology.

  • Motivates existing customers to visit boutiques or Authorized Dealers to have tokens issued for their items.

  • Provides manufacturers with information on indirect purchasers of their items.

Value to Consumer

  • Provides a reseller with a certificate of authenticity which cannot be counterfeited.

  • Organizes receipts, warranties, links to manuals, images, specifications, etc. in one secure place while providing alerts for recommended service intervals.

  • Enables the ability to virtually show a collection to others via mobile devices.

  • Connects parties interested in purchasing out of production models with potential sellers of those items.
